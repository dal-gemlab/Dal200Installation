FIRST WEBSITE DESIGN PROJECT

She was born in 1998 in Kozhikode (Calicut), India. When she was 3 years old, she drew pictures using MS Paint. By the age of 8 she was entrusted with the work of developing a website for her school.

YOUNGEST TITLE

The Association of American Webmasters honored her by granting her membership along with their highest award for excellence in web design, the Gold Web Award. She also received the National Child Award for Exceptional Achievement in 2008, the highest honor in India for Children instituted the by Government of India.

THE YOUNGEST CEO

Sreelakshmi started her web designing company, eDesign Technologies. At the age of 11, she had already designed and developed more than 100 websites for various institutions and organizations, including the Bar Council of Kerala, Angels International, EHP India, and Mammas Passion.

VENTURE

Her first venture was TinyLogo, a logo based search engine. She also started another firm, Online Pixel Traders.
