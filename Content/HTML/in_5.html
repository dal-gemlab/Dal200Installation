HUMAN ERROR

One of her key contributions was to add code to the Apollo flight Software to account for human error. This code stopped a system overload when a crew member set a switch in the wrong position. The software handled the error as intended, and the astronauts successfully landed on the moon.

AWARDS

She was awarded the NASA Exceptional Space Act Award for her scientific and technical contributions. She was chosen as a recipient of the Presidential Medal of Freedom by U.S. President Barack Obama.
